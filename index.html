Short Bio

Bo Tang is currently a PhD student in Department of Computer Science and Institute for Cyber Security at the University of Texas at San Antonio. His doctoral research advisor is Dr. Ravi Sandhu. His research interests focus on multi-tenant collaborative access control for cloud services. Tang received a Bachelor of Engineering degree in Information Security from Huazhong University of Science and Technology.
