Short Bio

Bo Tang is currently a PhD candidate in Department of Computer Science and Institute for Cyber Security at the University of Texas at San Antonio. His doctoral research advisor is Dr. Ravi Sandhu. His dissertation is titled Multi-Tenant Access Control for Collaborative Cloud Services. Tang possesses 10+ years of experience in information security including 3-year working experience as an information security engineer in a Forbes 500 company. He has a B.E. in Information Security and a B.A. in English from Huazhong University of Science and Technology.
